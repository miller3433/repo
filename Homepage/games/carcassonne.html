Carcassonne: Bridges, Castles, and Bazaars

  • 2-6 players
  • 30-45 Minutes
  • Ages 8+

Description

The eighth expansion for Carcassonne, Br√ºcken, Burgen und Basare (English: Bridges, Castles & Bazaars) allows players three new options while playing: Bridges may be used to continue a road over a field tile, thereby allowing an incomplete road to technically run into a field segment. Bazaars are a new type of tile that allows players to auction off tiles in exchange for points. Part of the Carcassonne series.
